Privacy policy

Warp Weft privacy policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026.

Summary

Warp Weft does not require users to create an external account and does not run a third-party backend for its core feature. Pattern generation happens locally in the user's browser inside Canva.

Information we handle

  • pattern settings entered by the user, such as colors and repeat counts
  • generated repeat sheet images created locally for insertion into Canva
  • support emails voluntarily sent to qmemp@naver.com

How data is used

User-entered pattern settings are used only to generate the repeat sheet requested by the user. Generated images are uploaded to Canva only when the user explicitly chooses to add the repeat sheet to their design.

Data sharing

Warp Weft does not sell personal information and does not share personal data with third-party advertising providers. Canva may process content and account data according to Canva's own policies when users work inside the Canva platform.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent to qmemp@naver.com.
