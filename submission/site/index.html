Warp Weft

Create woven repeat sheets inside Canva

Warp Weft is a Canva app for building plaid, twill, satin, and basket-weave textures directly in the editor. Adjust warp and weft colors, tune the weave structure, and place a ready-to-use repeat sheet into your design in one step.

What it does

Create textile-inspired repeat sheets for:

  • fashion moodboards
  • packaging mockups
  • editorial backgrounds
  • surface design concepts

Key capabilities:

  • curated weaving presets
  • editable warp and weft thread colors
  • repeat sheet controls
  • direct insertion into Canva designs
